Heparin Dilution Reference
Preparation: Use 5000 units/mL preparation
Dilute 1 mL in 49 mL NS or D5W
= 5,000 units in 50 mL = 100 units/mL

Bolus: Use undiluted 5000 units/mL
Volume (mL) = prescribed units / 5000

Discard diluted solution 24 hours after preparation.
Low Dose aPTT Adjustment Table
Monitoring Reminders

Baseline: FBC, PT/aPTT before starting (if not done within 72h).

aPTT >40s at baseline: Check with doctor before starting.

First aPTT: 6 hours after initiating infusion.

FBC: 48h after starting, then every 2-3 days up to 14 days (HIT surveillance).

Blood sampling: Do NOT draw aPTT from same limb receiving heparin.

Major bleeding: Stop infusion, check aPTT immediately.

About Adjusted Body Weight

For BMI >40, adjusted body weight (ABW) avoids supratherapeutic dosing.

ABW = IBW + 0.4 x (Actual - IBW)

IBW (Devine):
Male: 50 + 2.3 x (height in inches - 60)
Female: 45.5 + 2.3 x (height in inches - 60)

EPIC auto-calculates ABW for height >152 cm. For height <=152 cm, contact Pharmacy.
